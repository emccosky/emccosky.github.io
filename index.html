MY "DAY JOB"

I am currently a senior at Vandegrift High School looking to pursue a degree in Computer Science and Business in college. I am involved in FIRST Robotics, Computer Science UIL, and USAF's CyberPatriot competition.

movie

MY FILM MAKING

A lot of my free time, and plenty of non-free time too, is spent working on filming and editing videos. Most of them are done for my FIRST robotics team in the form of event recaps, weekly vlogs, and more cinematic design reveals.

MY COMPANY

In the summer of 2016, I started a small LLC with several friends that specializes in working with small businesses to increase their branding and internet presence. We do graphic design, full stack web development, and make simple mobile apps for clients.
