WHO I AM


"I want to do something. Not just anything, but something big."

I want to be able to leave the world in better shape I inherited it in. But I'm not there yet. I'm taking things one chapter at a time, working towards this purpose that I have bestowed upon the book that is my life.

I am a born and raised Texan, a place where everything from the stars at night to the capitol building is bigger and brighter. I am pasionate about always improving, learning new technologies and trying new things. My interests include product design, marketing, data analytics, artificial intelligence, computer vision, film, and disruptive ideas.

I want to change the world, and wont settle until I do.
