WHO I AM

I want to do something. Not just anything, but something big. Something that will systematically better the lives of millions around the globe. Something that will allow me to leave the world in a better shape than the one I inherited it in. But I'm not there yet. I'm taking things one chapter at a time, working towards this purpose that I have bestowed upon the book that is my life.

I am pasionate about always improving, learning new technologies and trying new things. I want to change the world, and wont settle until I do.
